# Public Perception Post Angles

## Architecture Reveal

Most AI tools collapse strategy into one answer.

NEXUS splits it into specialized cognition:

- Researcher
- Architect
- Strategist
- Critic
- Memory Agent
- Orchestrator

The goal is not more text. The goal is a better thinking process.

Design an AI-native healthcare platform for reducing diagnostic errors.

NEXUS is not an AGI fantasy.

agents, debate, memory, and synthesis inside one workflow.

## Critic Feature Post

The most important agent in NEXUS might be the Critic.

It looks for:

- unrealistic assumptions
- weak GTM logic
- regulatory risk
- technical bottlenecks
- missing incentives

Good AI should not just help you move faster. It should stop you from being confidently wrong.
